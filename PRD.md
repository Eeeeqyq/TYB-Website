# TYB Website PRD (v1 Static Launch)

## Product Objective
Launch a credible corporate website for TYB International that supports partner trust, brand visibility, and inbound business inquiries.

## Primary Users
- Industrial buyers evaluating suppliers and trading partners.
- Regional suppliers and logistics partners assessing credibility.
- Investment and cross-border commerce contacts discovering TYB.

## User Needs
- Understand what TYB does, where it operates, and what it trades.
- Assess trust signals quickly (location, sectors, long-term positioning).
- Contact TYB through a clear and frictionless channel.

## Success Metrics (First 60 Days)
- Website is indexed by Google for brand queries.
- Homepage appears for branded search terms.
- Search Console shows valid indexing coverage for homepage.
- At least one measurable weekly inbound contact intent (email click or direct inquiry).

## Scope (v1)
- Single-page, static marketing website.
- English + Chinese content toggle already present in UI.
- Core sections: About, Sectors, Network, Sustainability, Contact.
- Basic technical SEO: metadata, canonical, Open Graph/Twitter tags, sitemap, robots, Organization schema.

## Out of Scope (v1)
- Multi-page CMS/blog.
- Dynamic lead forms/CRM integration.
- Authenticated experiences and backend APIs.
- Advanced analytics attribution modeling.

## Content and Approval Ownership
- Business copy owner: TYB team.
- Final legal/company naming and contact details: TYB management.
- Technical implementation owner: website maintainer/developer.

## Risks and Assumptions
- Domain may not be purchased yet at implementation time, so canonical may temporarily use the GitHub Pages URL.
- Social preview quality depends on a stable OG image asset and final domain URL.
- Future Next.js migration must preserve URL structure and metadata parity to avoid SEO loss.

## Release Criteria
- Site deployed over HTTPS.
- Custom domain connected (or temporary GitHub Pages domain active).
- Search Console property verified.
- Sitemap submitted and fetchable.
- Core metadata validated in live HTML source.
